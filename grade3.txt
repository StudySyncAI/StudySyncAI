Strong Narrative Writing Sample:
The old treehouse felt like a secret castle. One afternoon, my friend Leo and I decided to make it our spy headquarters. We wrote a secret code and made a password, "Dragonfly." We used binoculars to watch the "enemy" (my neighbor's cat). Suddenly, we heard rustling in the bushes below! We held our breath, but it was just my mom bringing us cookies and lemonade. Even spies need a snack break.

Strong Informative Writing Sample:
Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for playful behavior. They are not fish; they breathe air through a blowhole. Dolphins use echolocation to navigate and find food. They send high-pitched sounds that bounce off objects, telling location, size, and shape. They live in social groups called pods.
